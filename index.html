Description

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is a part of a 10-12 survival horror game series, where the player, you, is playing as a security guard. You must survive 5 nights while trying to survive the roaming animatronics that are out to kill you. You must wind a music box, watch cameras, flash the center hallway and 2 vents to keep the animatronics out. This game uses skill to get past the difficulty of the game getting more difficult as the nights continue. This game is known for the increased difficulty than the first game, new characters and many new mechanics to get used to. Can you survive 5 nights?
