SUMMARY

"NO PLACE TO RUN and exactly one place to hide."

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 is apart of a 10-12 horror game series. It is an independent
point-and-click survival game.

The old, aging, and broken original cast is now joined by a brand new set of characters.
Very kid-friendly and a new facial recognition tech! What could go wrong?

As the new night guard of this establishment, you have to make sure nothing goes wrong
during after-hours. The previous guard had many complaints about the animatronics
getting into the office. To make your job easier, Fazbear Entertainment has provided you with
your own empty Freddy head! The characters should be able to leave you alone once you
put on the mask. With their facial recognition code, they should recognize you as one of
them. Surely you will be fine!

As always, Fazbear Entertainment is not responsible for any death or dismemberment.
Stay safe, and have the best 5 nights of your life!
