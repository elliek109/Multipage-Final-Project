Five Nights at Freddy's 2 Characters

CHARACTERS

Here are all the characters that make appearances in the game.

Toy Animatronics

Toy Freddy

Toy Freddy is one of the main toy animatronics

Toy Bonnie

Toy Bonnie is one of the main toy animatronics

Toy Chica

Toy Chica is one of the main toy animatronics

Mangle

Mangle is one of the main toy animatronics, but is a little messed up.

Balloon Boy

Balloon Boy is a side animatronic, he only shows up when you run out of batteries.

The Puppet

The Puppet only shows up when you don't wind the music box, make sure you do that!

Withered Animatronics

Withered Freddy

Withered Freddy is one of the main discarded animatronics used for parts.

Withered Bonnie

Withered Bonnie is one of the main discarded animatronics used for parts.

Withered Chica

Withered Chica is one of the main discarded animatronics used for parts.

Withered Foxy

Withered Foxy is one of the main discarded animatronics used for parts.

Withered Golden Freddy

Withered Golden Freddy is a withered animatronic that has a rare chance of occurring.

Main Characters

Phone Guy

The guy over the phone that gives you tips every night to survive.

Michael Afton

One of the members of the Afton Family, working at the location to uncover secrets for William. He deals with the consequences of his family's legacy.

William Afton (Purple Guy)

The main murderer of the children that are stuck inside animatronics.

Henry Emily

The co-creator of Fazbear's animatronics, past co-worker with William, and the father of Charlotte Emily.
